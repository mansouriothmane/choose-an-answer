Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it safe to eat expired food if it doesn't smell bad?

A: No, expiration dates are put in place to indicate when a food item may no longer be safe to eat, even if it does not smell bad.

Q: Is it safe to sleep with my eyes open?

A: It is not possible to sleep with your eyes open as the muscles that control the eyelids relax during sleep, causing them to close.

Q: Is it safe to eat raw chicken if I marinate it first?

A: No, marinating raw chicken does not make it safe to eat as the bacteria that can cause foodborne illness can still be present.

Q: Can I use my car engine to power my house during a blackout?

A: No, attempting to use a car engine to power your house can be dangerous and may damage both the car and the house.
